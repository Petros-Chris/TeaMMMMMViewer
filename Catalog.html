Bai Hao Yin Zhen

Exquisite Chinese White Tea

Bai Hao Yin Zhen, also known as Silver Needle, is a prized Chinese white tea made from handpicked buds. Its delicate flavor and floral notes offer a refreshing, subtly sweet taste. Minimal processing highlights its purity, making it a choice for those seeking a refined tea experience.

Type: White

Mo Li Long Zhu

Jasmine-Infused Green Tea

Mo Li Long Zhu, known as Jasmine Dragon Pearls, is a fragrant Chinese green tea. Hand-rolled into pearls, it unveils a soothing jasmine aroma when brewed, delivering a light and floral taste. Crafted with care by layering green tea leaves with jasmine blossoms, it offers an elegant and calming experience.

Type: Jasmine Green

Genmaicha

Japanese Green Tea Blend

Genmaicha, a traditional Japanese green tea, combines green tea leaves with toasted brown rice. This unique blend offers a nutty, roasted flavor profile with a hint of sweetness. The addition of rice lends a satisfyingly rich and aromatic quality to the tea.

Type: Green

Dong Ding

Exquisite Taiwanese Oolong

Dong Ding tea, a revered Taiwanese oolong, originates from the Dong Ding mountain region. Renowned for its tightly rolled leaves and distinct roasted aroma, this oolong offers a rich, smooth taste with floral undertones.

Type: Oolong

Golden Monkey

Refined Chinese Black Tea

Golden Monkey tea, a prized Chinese black tea, derives its name from the unique appearance of its twisted golden tips resembling monkey claws. This tea offers a full-bodied, malty flavor with hints of cocoa and honey.

Type: Black

Milk Jin Xuan

Creamy Taiwanese Oolong

Milk Jin Xuan tea, a Taiwanese oolong, is celebrated for its naturally creamy texture and smooth, buttery taste. Grown in the high mountains of Taiwan, this tea exudes a delightful milky aroma and offers a gentle, sweet flavor with floral notes.

Type: Oolong

Lao Ban Zhang

Lao Ban Zhang pu-erh tea hails from the ancient tea forests of Yunnan, China. Revered for its exceptional quality, this tea boasts a robust, earthy flavor with woody undertones and a velvety texture. Grown from ancient tea trees, Lao Ban Zhang is cherished for its unique character.

Organic Grade Matcha is a finely ground, vibrant green tea powder originating from Japan. Huo Shan Huang

Sencha

Sencha is a classic Japanese green tea known for its vibrant green color and refreshing taste. Harvested from the top leaves of the tea plant, Sencha offers a grassy, slightly vegetal flavor with a clean, invigorating aroma.
